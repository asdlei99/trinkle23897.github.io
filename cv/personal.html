Jiayi Weng

Ph.D. Student, Tsinghua University
Email: duchao0726 at gmail dot com

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student of TSAIL Group in Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University. I am advised by Prof. Jun Zhu and Prof. Bo Zhang. Before that, I received my B.Eng. from the Yao Class in Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences at Tsinghua University.

My research interests include deep learning algorithms and applications, especially the intersection of deep learning and statistical machine learning methods. I develop learning and (Bayesian) inference methods for deep models and apply them to real-world tasks ranging from computer vision to recommendation systems.
